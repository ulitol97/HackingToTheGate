override void Game.Entities.Enemies.PatrolEnemy.CheckDistance ( )
protectedvirtual

Computes the distance between the enemy and its target (target) and updates the enemy's position in case the target is in the chase radius but not in the attack radius and the enemy is not staggered.

It also handles the animations in case the enemy wakes up or goes to sleep.
